My Timeline

Sep 2023 – Ongoing

FYP - Central Banking Digital Currency Transaction Processor

My final year project, "Central Banking Transaction Processor," involved creating a private blockchain using Hyperledger Fabric for secure financial transactions. I implemented the SHA-256 and RSA-256 hashing algorithms on a Zybo FPGA, ensuring robust security and performance. Additionally, I managed the installation of the operating system, scheduling, and interfacing on the Zybo, demonstrating my technical proficiency in blockchain and embedded systems.

Jan 2023 – May 2024

Lead - COMPPEC 2024

I served as the Lead Organizer for COMPPEC (Computer Project Computation and Exhibition), a prominent national-level event hosted by the Department of Computer and Software Engineering at NUST College of EME. The event spanned three days, from May 10 to May 12, and attracted participants from across Pakistan. In this role, I was responsible for overseeing the planning, coordination, and execution of the event, ensuring a seamless experience for attendees and participants. This position allowed me to demonstrate my leadership skills and ability to manage large-scale projects effectively.

June 2023 – Sep 2023

Intern - Imagage

I have completed my internship at ImagAge Inc., a renowned New York-based company. During this time, I contributed to a project titled "TT Scorer," a web application designed for organizations and players to register and record their match scores. My role primarily focused on developing the front end using ReactJS, and I also managed the database using MySQL. This opportunity allowed me to enhance my skills in cutting-edge technologies while delivering innovative solutions

Feb 2022 – May 2022

Intern - AIRLIFT Technologies

As an IoT Engineer at Air Lift, I contributed to the implementation of an automatic lock system for delivery cabins using a Node MCU and uploaded code on it. In my role as an Embedded Systems Engineer at Air Lift, I supported the development of interconnected systems that improved delivery efficiency and security. My work at Air Lift provided valuable experience in hardware and software integration while contributing to the enhancement of the delivery process.

Dec 2021- June 2022

Deputy Director of Graphic Design - COMPPEC, NUSt College of EME.

As Deputy Director of Graphics Designing at COMPPEC, I led and managed the graphics design team in creating visually appealing designs for the event's promotional material. I collaborated with other team members to create designs for various marketing materials and oversaw the printing and production process. Additionally, I ensured the team worked efficiently and all design work was completed on time. I also maintained consistency with the event's theme and brand identity in all designs created by the team.
